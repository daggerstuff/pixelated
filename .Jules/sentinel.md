## 2024-05-18 - Fix XSS in ChatMessage.tsx | Vulnerability: Unsanitized markdown-to-HTML conversion injected via dangerouslySetInnerHTML | Learning: Custom simple markdown parsers often miss XSS vectors, leaving the app vulnerable to script injection. | Prevention: Always wrap output of markdown-to-HTML functions with an isomorphic sanitizer like isomorphic-dompurify when passing to dangerouslySetInnerHTML.
